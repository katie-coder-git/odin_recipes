Ribeye Steak

Description

Restaurant Quality Ribeye Steak with Veggies in less than 30 mins!

Ingredients

  • Ribeye Steak 10oz
  • French Green Beans
  • Carrots
  • Salt, Pepper, Grape Seed Oil

Steps

  1. Season meat with salt and pepper and leave in room temperature for at least 30 mins.
  2. Chop and saute green beans and carrots
  3. Heat a pan before cooking the meat
  4. Sear the meet on the smoking hot cast iron pan with grape seed oil for 3 min
  5. Fip the meat and cook additional 3 min
  6. Take out the meat from the pan and leave for 1 min
  7. Plate the meat with sauted veggies
  8. Serve with French Baguette
