Thai Sweet Basil

Description

Thai Comfort Food made with sauted ground pork and thai holy basils

Ingredients

  • Ground Pork 1b
  • Thai Pepper
  • Dried Red Pepper
  • Garlic 1 clove
  • Egg
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Chop 1 clove of garlic.
  2. Chop Thai Pepper and Dried Red Pepper
  3. Prepare for sauce by mixing Oyster Sauce, Soy Sauce, Fish Sauce, and sugar, each 1 Table Spoon.
  4. Heat a pan and roast chopped garlic with oil.
  5. Add chopped Peppers
  6. Add Ground Pork
  7. When pork is cooked, add the mixed sauce and lower the heat
  8. Add thai holy basil and cook for 1 more min
  9. Fry egg on high heat in sunny side up
  10. Serve with rice
