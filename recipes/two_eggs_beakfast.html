Two Eggs Breakfast

Description

American Simple Breakfast with Two Egges

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Bacons
  • Sausages
  • Hashed Potatoes
  • Sliced Bread

Steps

  1. Cook Sausages on a griddle
  2. Cook Bacons under a broiler of a oven
  3. Cook Hashed Potatos to the point of crispy surface
  4. Cook 2 Eggs in a preferred style
  5. Serve the cooked eggs, sausages, bacons, and hashed brown potatoes with toasted sliced bread
